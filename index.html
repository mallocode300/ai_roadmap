The Digital Product Spectrum

AI products range from low-barrier "side hustles" to high-investment SaaS platforms. The path you choose determines your upfront cost and potential for scalable income.

Low-Barrier Products

Quick to create, low upfront cost. Ideal for testing ideas and generating initial revenue. Think "side hustle."

Examples: AI Art on Etsy, Prompt Packs on Gumroad

Revenue: Modest, often requires active marketing ($200-$1000/mo profit)

Model: One-time purchases

High-Barrier Products

Requires significant time, skill, and capital. Offers the highest potential for recurring, scalable income. Think "entrepreneur."

Examples: Micro-SaaS, AI Wrappers, Full Platforms

Revenue: High potential, slow build (can scale to $10k+ MRR)

Model: Subscriptions, Usage-Based
